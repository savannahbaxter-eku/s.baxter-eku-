About Our School

Daniel Boone Elementary is a caring learning community where students are encouraged to explore, ask questions, and grow into confident readers and thinkers.

Our Library Goals

  • 📖 Encourage a lifelong love of reading
  • 📚 Help students find books that match their interests and reading levels
  • 🧠 Teach research and information skills
  • 🤝 Create a welcoming space for learning and discovery
  • 🌟 Support classroom learning through engaging resources

Library Mission

Our mission is to support students in becoming curious, confident, and independent learners by providing access to books, technology, and meaningful learning experiences.
