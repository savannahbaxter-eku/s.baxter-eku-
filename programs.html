📖 Reading Buddies

Older students help younger students practice reading skills. This program builds confidence, fluency, and friendships through shared reading.

📚 Book Checkout Days

Students visit the library weekly to check out books that match their interests and reading levels.

🌟 Reading Challenges

Students set reading goals and earn rewards for completing books and meeting milestones.

Digital Literacy Lessons

Students learn how to safely use technology, research topics, and evaluate online information.

Special Events
